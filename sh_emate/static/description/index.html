Emate - Email Theme

This theme helps you to take your email marketing to another level. See if this sounds familiar? You keep running into problems like outmoded email design, and incompetence to incorporate your email into a true cross-platform experience. Here in this theme you have lots of options to design your emails like pro. Here you can get more than 20 well crafted ready-made email templates for different 9 business model. In this theme you can get more than 100 snippets which help to design emails related to your brand. You can make it truly relevant With the E-mate themes.

Dynamic Product

We have created a feature that is dynamic products. where you can set products on snippets on a single click. Just define the product in ‘Dynamic product’. No more technical skills required.

Dynamic Event

We have created a feature for the event is to set events dynamically on email templates. You need to just define events list in ‘Dynamic Event’, select appropriate snippet and select that event into snippet configuration.

Dynamic Blog

We have created a feature for the blog is to set blogs dynamically on email templates. You need to just define blogs list in ‘Dynamic Blog’, select appropriate snippet and select that blog into snippet configuration.

20+ Ready Template

Here we will provide ready-made email templates with more than 7 different business model. There are more than 21 different well-crafted responsive email template. You can easily use that template, just drag and drop that's it.

Advance Snippet Tools

In this theme, you can edit snippet with advance snippet tool. There are lots of option for decorating your snippets like border, box shadow with custom settings, background colour, number of columns, etc.

100+ Snippets

In this theme, we will provide more than 20 different categories of snippets with more than 100 different styles so, You easily use snippets across the email templates. You can easily craft your email template like a pro.

Dynamic Brand Colour

Brand color is the secret in producing a good identity for a company and marketing because colors convey emotions and experiences. You can easily set the brand colour on email template. Just go to ‘Mass Mailing Theme Settings’, Select appropriate colours and go for it.
